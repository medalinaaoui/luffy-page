Luffy's life timeline:

  • Early Childhood: Luffy was born in Foosha Village, Dawn Island. At the age of seven, he was inspired by the pirate Red-Haired Shanks and developed his dream of becoming the Pirate King.
  • Encounter with Shanks: Luffy accidentally ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi, a Devil Fruit that granted him rubber powers but made him unable to swim. He formed a strong bond with Shanks and his crew during his time with them.
  • Sabo's Disappearance: Luffy's close friend, Sabo, mysteriously vanished, leaving a lasting impact on him and his determination to achieve his dream.
  • Setting Sail: At the age of seventeen, Luffy sets off on his journey as a pirate, forming the Straw Hat Pirates crew and wearing the iconic straw hat given to him by Shanks.
  • Grand Line Adventure: Luffy and his crew navigate the treacherous Grand Line, facing formidable enemies like the Warlords of the Sea, the Navy, and other powerful pirates.
  • Enies Lobby: Luffy leads his crew in a fierce battle against the World Government to rescue their crewmate Nico Robin, showing his unwavering loyalty to his friends.
  • Two-Year Time Skip: Luffy and his crew disband for two years to train and become stronger, with each member pursuing their own personal growth.
  • Return and Confrontation: Luffy reunites with his crew, now more powerful, and challenges the world again, clashing with powerful foes like the Yonko and the Marines.
  • Whole Cake Island: Luffy faces off against Charlotte Linlin (Big Mom) on Whole Cake Island to rescue his crewmate Sanji, showcasing his determination and willpower.
  • Wano Country: Luffy enters Wano Country to take down Kaido and free the nation from his oppressive rule, leading a massive alliance to confront the Yonko.
  • Path to Pirate King: Luffy continues his journey towards Raftel, the final island of the Grand Line, where the One Piece, the ultimate treasure, is said to be hidden, as he strives to become the Pirate King.
